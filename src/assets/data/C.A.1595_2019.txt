The process server’s report states that the appellant’s brother, Mr. Bilal Soori , refused to receive notice on his behalf. It has become quite clear that the appellant is avoiding service of notice which conduct , by a former parliamentarian and Deputy Speaker of the National Assembly , is unfortu nate. 2.
